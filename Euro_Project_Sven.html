Introduction

In the dynamic world of soccer, where every pass, sprint, and strategic move can influence the outcome of a game, the distance covered by a team throughout a match doesnt emerge as a pivotal metric. The modern game demands a a blend of skill, teamwork, and relentless physical effort. The distance traveled on the field is not merely a statistical figure but a reflection of the collective energy, endurance, and tactical acumen of the players.

Questions

Does running around the field more prompt you to shoot more?

Did running more effect who the tournaments winner was?

Summary

There are many important stats that can show why a team has more success than another team. My goal was to present a stat not so heavily represented and show how it can effect an outcome. I conclude that it isnt as conclusive as I had thought, but in the case of the 2020 European championship the team with the highest average distance ran won the tournament.
